Our Story

Born from a Passion for Real Food

Cafe Lottle started as a simple dream — a cozy spot where students from DG Vaishnav College and residents of Arumbakkam could enjoy honest, delicious food without burning a hole in their pockets.

We opened our doors in 2022, and since then, we've been on a mission to bring warmth, good taste, and community together. Our filter kaapi is brewed the traditional way, our biryani is cooked on slow dum, and every dish is made with the care of a home kitchen.

From a single counter to a full menu of drinks, bites, shakes, and biryanis — the journey has been fuelled by your love and our relentless passion for great food.
